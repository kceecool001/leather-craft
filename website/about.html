About Leather Craft Ventures

Experts in Apparel & Leather Manufacturing since 2013

Our Story

Crafting Excellence

Leather Craft Ventures Limited is a proudly Nigerian, family-owned business with over 30 years of experience in the manufacturing industry. Established in Ikeja, Lagos, we started as a small operation built on quality craftsmanship and a commitment to excellence. Over the years, we've grown into a trusted name, serving schools, organizations, and individuals across Nigeria.

Leather Craft Ventures Limited is a legally registered company under Nigerian law (RC NO: 415897). Led by our Managing Director, the business has remained family-driven, upholding the values of integrity, hard work, and attention to detail. From producing premium leather boots and shoes to crafting school uniforms, monogrammed apparel, and knitted collars, we pride ourselves on delivering durable, high-quality solutions tailored to meet diverse needs.

At Leather Craft Ventures Limited, our legacy is built on strong relationships, consistency, and a passion for creating value. Every product that leaves our facility reflects our unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship and the understanding that our clients depend on us for products that represent their brand and values.
