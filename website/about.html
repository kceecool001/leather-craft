About Leather Craft Ventures

Experts in Apparel & Leather Manufacturing since our founding in Nigeria

Our Story

Founded in Arepo, Ogun State

Leather Craft Ventures Ltd was established in Arepo, Ogun State, with a vision to become Nigeria's premier manufacturer of quality leather goods and corporate apparel. From our humble beginnings, we have grown to serve major institutions, hotels, security firms, and educational establishments across the country.

Our specialisation spans four core product lines: premium leather boots built for durability and comfort, school uniforms tailored to institutional specifications, precision-knitted collars, and custom monogramming services that help brands establish their identity.

Every product that leaves our facility reflects our unwavering commitment to quality craftsmanship, attention to detail, and the understanding that our clients depend on us for products that represent their brand and values.

"Crafting products that combine exceptional craftsmanship with lasting comfort"
