Our Services

Four product lines built on precision and expertise

Premium Leather Boots

Our handcrafted leather boots are built for professionals who demand both durability and comfort. Using premium leather sourced from trusted suppliers, each pair is constructed with reinforced stitching, cushioned insoles, and slip-resistant soles. We specialise in bulk orders for security firms, hospitality staff, and corporate uniforms.

Available in multiple styles including ankle boots, knee-high boots, and safety boots with steel toe caps. Custom sizing, colour matching, and logo embossing available for institutional orders. Our boots are designed to withstand daily professional use while maintaining their appearance and comfort.

Typical clients include hotels, security companies, military contractors, and corporate organisations requiring professional footwear for their staff. Minimum order quantities apply for custom specifications.

School Uniforms

We manufacture complete school uniform sets tailored to each institution's specific requirements and brand guidelines. Our uniforms combine traditional craftsmanship with modern fabric technology to ensure comfort, durability, and easy maintenance. Every garment is cut and sewn to precise measurements with consistent quality control throughout production.

Our uniform range includes shirts, blouses, trousers, skirts, blazers, cardigans, and accessories. We work with schools to develop custom designs, incorporate school colours and crests, and ensure all uniforms meet the institution's dress code standards. Fabrics are selected for their durability, colour-fastness, and comfort in Nigeria's climate.

Primary clients include private schools, international schools, and educational institutions requiring high-quality uniforms for their students and staff. We handle orders from small batches to large institutional contracts with flexible delivery schedules.

Knitted Collars

Our precision-knitted collars are crafted to complement any uniform or garment line with professional finishing and attention to detail. Using advanced knitting techniques and quality yarns, we produce collars that maintain their shape, colour, and texture through repeated washing and wear. Each collar is designed to integrate seamlessly with your existing apparel.

Available in various styles including ribbed collars, flat collars, polo collars, and custom designs. We offer multiple yarn weights, colour options, and pattern variations to match your specific requirements. Our knitting process ensures consistent quality and precise measurements across large production runs.

Ideal for uniform manufacturers, fashion brands, and institutions requiring custom collar solutions for their garments. We work closely with designers and manufacturers to develop collar specifications that enhance the overall garment design and functionality.

Monogramming & Customisation

Transform your apparel into branded corporate identity pieces with our precision embroidery and monogramming services. Using state-of-the-art embroidery machines and quality threads, we add logos, names, initials, and custom designs to garments with professional precision. Our customisation services help organisations establish their brand identity through their uniforms and corporate wear.

Services include logo embroidery, name badges, rank insignia, decorative stitching, and custom patches. We work with vector artwork, provide design consultation, and offer colour matching to ensure your branding is accurately represented. Our embroidery is designed to withstand commercial washing and maintain its appearance over time.

Perfect for hotels requiring staff name badges, security firms needing rank identification, schools wanting house emblems, and corporations establishing uniform branding programmes. We handle both new garment customisation and adding branding to existing apparel.
